        Professional Training

        Full time employees will get one week of Training paid per year.  
        This is an opportunity to step away from your normal week and learn about a new skill or technology.
        We have contracted New Horizons for this as they offer a good mix of Programming, Database, Security, Infrastructure, Networking, and Project Management Classes.  
        They also have regional offices and offer remote class room experiences so you do not have to travel to Indy to take advantage of this opportunity.

            Work with your Group Manager to plan your time and course selection

        Optional Benefit

            We offer $1000 every calendar year if you pass an exam (Exam must be an approved by Testing Management).  This includes Project Management courses.

            We can offer a $2500 Permanent Salary Increase for exams that requires passing a pretty significant accomplishment

            We will Pay for the Certification Test if you pass.  If you fail, it is your expense.  That may seem harsh but we want to people to be prepared.

         

        Examples for $1000 bonus: CCNA, MCP, PMP, Java SE 7 Programmer I, CSM (Certified Scrum Master), AWS Associate

        Examples for $2500 increase: MCSE, CCNP, CISSP, Java SE 7 Programmer II, CSP, AWS Professional

        Example: Achieving Java SE 7 Programmer II requires first passing Java SE 7 Programmer I. 
                If both are passed in 1 calendar year: The employee gets $1000 bonus for passing the first test and a $2500 raise for passing the second. (no $1000 bonus for the second because it was in a calendar year).
